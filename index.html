About Myself

Hi there! I am currently a Ph.D. Candidate of National University of Defense Technology. I joined Trustie & OSSEAN Group at August 2013, advised by Prof. Huaimin Wang. Before that I graduated with a B.E. in Computer Science and Technology from National University of Defense Technology in 2013. My main research interest is empirical software engineering, spanning from mining software repositories and analysing social coding networks. Now I am visiting and studying in DECAL Lab, UC Davis, advised by Prof. Vladimir Filkov.
